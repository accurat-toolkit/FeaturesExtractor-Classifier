Last Sunday, 4 May 2008. Regional Kickboxing Championship held Istria in the disciplines of semi and light contact for all ages. Here&#39;s the report: On Sunday, 04.05.2008. in the Sports Hall in Bale, organized by the Association of Istrian County Kickboxing, Kickboxing Championship was held in the disciplines of Istria semi contact and light contact for all ages. Host this championship was the Kickboxing Club Budokai from Rovinj, led by Pero Miškoviæ. Performances by participants from eight Kickboxing Club, Kickboxing Association members Istria and KBK Planet Sport from Pula, the Pula Futura KBK, KBK Lion of Pula, Medulin, the Lion of KBK, KBK Budokai from Labin, KBK from Prime PoreË, KBK King of PoreË and KBK Budokai from Rovinj. Reported were the contestants from KBK Trojan Istra, Pula but did not come to the competition. The championship judges are judged HKBS-a: The River Peter Mikuliæ, D.
