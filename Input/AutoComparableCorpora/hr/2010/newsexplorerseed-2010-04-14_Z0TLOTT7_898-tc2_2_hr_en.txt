Katyn (Russian: Katyn, Polish: Katy≈Ñ) is a village in Smolenskoj field in Russia. Contents [edit] Geography Katyn is located about 20 km west of Smolensk, and about 60 km from the border with Belarus is one of the stations on the international railway line Berlin-Warsaw-Minsk-Moscow. [Edit] Population According to the census of 2002. The Katyn had 1737 inhabitants. [Edit] History More detailed article: The massacre in the woods Katynskoj [edit] The massacre in the woods Katynskoj The massacre in the woods Katynskoj units of the Soviet Ministry of Internal Affairs (known as the NKVD) murdered thousands of Polish officers.
