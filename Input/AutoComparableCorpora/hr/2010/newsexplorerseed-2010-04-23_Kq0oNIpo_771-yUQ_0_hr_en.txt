Azerbaijan (Azerbaijani: Azərbaycan), states in the Caucasus. The capital is Baku. It has an area of 86 600 square kilometers and a population of around 7.762 million. Independent state since 1991, and before that was a republic within the Soviet Union. Contents [edit - edit] Demography [edit - edit] Language Official language: Azerbaijani.
