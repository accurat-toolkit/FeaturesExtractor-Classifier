Hippotragus equinus is a mammal of the order Cetartiodactyla the family Bovidae. Contents [edit] Threats This species is not endangered, and referred to as the last concern because it has a wide distribution. [2] [edit] Distribution of range of this species Hippotragus equinus includes a number of countries in Africa. The species is present in Swaziland (reintroduced), Sudan, Mauritania, Mali, Niger, Nigeria, Cameroon, DR Congo, Mozambique, Ethiopia, Zambia, Zimbabwe, South Africa, Angola, Kenya, Tanzania, Benin, Botswana, Burkina Faso, Centralnoafričkoj Republic, Chad, Côte d&#39;Ivoire, Ghana, Guinea Bissau, Malawi, Namibia, Rwanda, Senegal, Togo and Uganda.
