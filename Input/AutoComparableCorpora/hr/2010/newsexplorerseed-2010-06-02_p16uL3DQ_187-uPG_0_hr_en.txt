On Sunday, 14.02.2010. in the city&#39;s sports arena in Pula PATTINAGGIO, organized by the Association of Istrian County Kickboxing, Kickboxing Championship was held in the County of Istria disciplines semi contact and light contact for all ages. Host this championship was the Kickboxing Club &quot;Lion&quot; from Pula. Reported are 336 contestants in the categories of boys and girls to seniors in the semi contact and of the older cadets to seniors in light contact. At three venues have been worked 233 fights, 168 of them in the fight semi contact fighting, and 65 in light contact. This is so far the largest number of appearances on the Istrian County kickboxing championship in the last five years showing tremendous growth popularity of this sport. Performances by participants from eight Kickboxing Club, Kickboxing Association members of the County of Istria and KBK &quot;Budokai Labin&quot; in Labin, KBK &quot;Budokai&quot; in Rovinj, KBK &quot;Futura&quot; from Pula, KBK &quot;Lion&quot; from Pula, KBK &quot;Lion&quot; from Medulin ; KBK &quot;Planet sport&quot; in Pula, KBK &quot;Prime&quot; from PoreË and KBK &quot;Trojan Istra&quot; Pula.
